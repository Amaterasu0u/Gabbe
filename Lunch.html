Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 bay leaves

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic for 30 minutes.
  2. Simmer the marinated chicken with the marinade, bay leaves, and pepper until cooked.
  3. Reduce the sauce until slightly thickened.

Season: Great all year round, especially during the rainy season.

Pork Sinigang

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly
  • 2 medium tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 10 cups water
  • 1 pack tamarind soup base
  • 2 cups assorted vegetables (okra, eggplant, kangkong)

Instructions:

  1. Boil pork, tomatoes, and onion in water until tender.
  2. Add the tamarind soup base and vegetables.
  3. Simmer until vegetables are cooked.

Season: Best during the rainy season for a warm, tangy soup.

Kare-Kare

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg oxtail (or beef tripe)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup toasted rice flour
  • 2 cups assorted vegetables (eggplant, pechay, sitaw)
  • 3 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Boil the oxtail until tender.
  2. Add peanut butter and rice flour slurry to thicken the sauce.
  3. Simmer with vegetables until cooked.

Season: Perfect for special occasions, especially during the holiday season.

Laing

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups dried taro leaves
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 chili peppers

Instructions:

  1. Simmer coconut milk, garlic, and onion in a pot.
  2. Add dried taro leaves and cook until tender.
  3. Top with coconut cream and chili peppers before serving.

Season: Ideal during the rainy season when hearty, creamy dishes are comforting.

Bicol Express

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kg pork belly, sliced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 chili peppers, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Saute garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add pork and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add coconut milk, chili peppers, and simmer until thickened.

Season: Best enjoyed during colder months for its spicy, creamy flavors.

Bulalo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef shank with bone marrow
  • 1 corn, sliced
  • 1 cabbage, quartered
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 10 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Boil beef shank in water until tender.
  2. Add onion, corn, and simmer until the flavors blend.
  3. Add cabbage just before serving.

Season: Perfect for cold weather or rainy days for its warm, hearty soup.
