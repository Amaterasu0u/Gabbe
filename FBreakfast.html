Croissant

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 2/3 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Prepare dough and fold in butter layers.
  2. Shape into crescents, proof, and bake until golden brown.

Season: Perfect year-round.

Pain au Chocolat

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 100g dark chocolate sticks

Instructions:

  1. Roll out dough, place chocolate sticks, and fold.
  2. Proof and bake until golden brown.

Season: Great for winter mornings.

French Toast (Pain Perdu)

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of stale bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Butter for frying

Instructions:

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, and sugar.
  2. Dip bread and fry in butter until golden.

Season: Best in spring with fresh fruits.

Crepes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients into a thin batter.
  2. Cook in a nonstick pan until lightly browned.

Season: Best enjoyed in summer with fresh berries.

Omelette au Fromage

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for frying

Instructions:

  1. Whisk eggs, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook in butter, add cheese, and fold.

Season: Perfect for year-round breakfasts.
