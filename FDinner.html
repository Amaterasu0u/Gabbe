Chateaubriand

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lb beef tenderloin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • 1 cup red wine
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season beef with salt, pepper, and thyme.
  3. Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet. Brown beef on all sides.
  4. Transfer to oven and roast for 20 minutes for medium-rare.
  5. Let rest, then deglaze the skillet with red wine to make sauce.

Season: Best in winter or special occasions.

Poulet Provencal

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and brown chicken thighs on both sides.
  2. Add garlic, tomatoes, basil, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Simmer for 30 minutes until chicken is tender.

Season: Ideal for summer.

Salmon en Papillon

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 sprig fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place salmon fillets on parchment paper, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Top with lemon slices, butter, and dill. Fold parchment paper around fish to form a packet.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes or until salmon is cooked through.

Season: Perfect for spring and summer.

Tartiflette

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs potatoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 lb lardons (bacon cubes)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1 wheel of Reblochon cheese, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil potatoes until tender, drain, and set aside.
  3. Cook lardons and onions in a pan, then deglaze with wine.
  4. In a baking dish, layer potatoes, lardons, and onions. Top with cheese slices.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes until golden and bubbly.

Season: Best in winter.

Duck Confit

Ingredients:

  • 4 duck legs
  • 2 cups duck fat
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 thyme sprigs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Season duck legs with salt, pepper, and garlic.
  2. Cover with duck fat and cook slowly in a low oven for 3 hours.
  3. Serve with crispy potatoes or greens.

Season: Ideal for winter.

Tarte Tatin

Ingredients:

  • 6 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Caramelize sugar and butter in a pan, then add apples.
  2. Cover with puff pastry and bake until golden.

Season: Best in fall with apples in season.
